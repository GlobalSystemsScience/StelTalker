Stellarium "Drop-In Planetarium" project

See sky at any time, controlling
       -Date
       -Time of day/night
       -Diurnal Motion, controlling speed both Forward and Backward

Find Constellations
       --Select Constellation
       --Show Stick figure(s)
       --Show art version(s)
               -Indian
               -Greek/Roman
               -Chinese
               -other cultures
See sky from any location on Earth, selecting from
       -map
       -List Cities
       -Latitude/Longitude

Show Moon changes
       Select Current Date or Specify Date
       Show 1 month cycle in daily steps showing progression ofphases
               (effectively, Earth not rotating)
       Starting position:
               -right after sunset if Moon is waxing
               -right before sunrise if Moon is waning

Planets
       Mark position of planet on meridian
       Zoom to planet/Teleport to
       Stand on a moon to view planet

Starclock---Tell time by the stars
